Between Two; It Doesn't Rain

"Last night stopped me -
A man with green eyes.
He had a leather jacket -
And a smile you can't believe."

"Last night stoppeth me
Also someone -
And he sounds a bit like the
Guy from your story."


"He's the same!
It seems!
It's not happenstenance!"

"Boy-
Sit down!
And take heed-"

Between - two; It doesn't rain,
But, you also won't see sun!
You were playing with us both-
Didn't know that you would lose!

Between two; It doesn't rain.
With your luck you play!
You wanted us both.
With neither will you stay!
